About ML Boot-Camp:

OpenAlchemy has come up with an idea of organising this exuberating for all the enthusiasts, newcomers and veterans alike.This Boot-Camp is divided into 3 parts (This Boot-Camp will be held for a month on every weekend):

  • Every Saturday, we will have a general meet-up where all the attendees can solve their doubts, will carry out discussions on various topics and new research going on in this field, can do networking with each other and with the amazing mentors who’ll always be there to help them out.

  • Every Sunday, we will have 6 hours study-jams or else you can say the hands-on sessions, where attendees will learn everything right from the basics to the advanced level in the field of ML/AI/Data Science/Deep Learning etc.

  • And then after a one-month of discussion, solving doubts, carrying out hands-on practical sessions; the attendees will be given a time period of 2 weeks where they will build their own project from all the learning and experience that they have gained throughout the Boot-Camp and then after 2 weeks, they will showcase their projects which will be reviewed by the mentors. Even during this time period, the attendees can reach out to the mentors to ask any doubt or solve any problem.

So, basically our team OpenAlchemy has planned this ML Boot-Camp in such a way that it thoroughly moulds the attendees and then they will be put to the test like a fine shape of artistic model is placed in a kiln so that they come out solid and can excel in this beautiful field of ML/AI-verse.
